About TechCon 2024

Discover the legacy, mission, and vision behind the world's premier technology conference

Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in a university auditorium. Founded by visionary entrepreneur Elena Rodriguez, the conference aimed to create a space where emerging technologies could be discussed openly.

Over the past 14 years, TechCon has evolved into a global phenomenon, hosting over 50,000 attendees annually. From humble beginnings, we've grown to become the world's most influential technology conference, with events held across four continents.

Our journey has mirrored the evolution of technology itself - from the early days of mobile computing to today's era of AI and quantum breakthroughs. Each year, we've pushed boundaries, introduced groundbreaking concepts, and connected innovators who have gone on to shape our digital future.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to accelerate technological progress by connecting brilliant minds across disciplines and industries. We believe that the most transformative innovations emerge at the intersection of diverse perspectives.

We're committed to creating an inclusive platform where established leaders and emerging talents can share knowledge, challenge assumptions, and collaborate on solutions to humanity's greatest challenges.
